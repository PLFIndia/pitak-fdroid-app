First public release of the Pitak F-Droid edition.

* Local-first personal book library: owned books, wishlist, and encrypted lending records.
* ISBN and QR scanning via ZXing — no proprietary dependencies.
* Cover capture with the system camera and an open-source cropper.
* Encrypted, biometric-gated borrower and loan data.
* Encrypted backup and restore.
* Export to JSON/CSV and publish a self-contained web catalogue.
* No telemetry, no account, no developer server.
